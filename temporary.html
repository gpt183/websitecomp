Welcome to Polytechnic College

VISION To impart quality and value based education in the field of Diploma Engineering so as to produce competent Technocrats to meet industry requirements with societal and environmental concern and ethical values.

MISSION
To impart technical knowledge and skills by using modern engineering and e-learning tools.
To facilitate Industry-Institute interaction to expose students to current industrial practices that will help them to solve industrial challenges
To inculcate ethical and professional values among students that will make them socially and environmentally responsible.
To motivate students towards lifelong learning and helping them to find right career opportunities.

Our Departments

CME - Computer Engineering

Focuses on engineering principles and design for the software technology.

ECE - Electronics and Communication Engineering

Specializes in communication systems, electronics, and signal processing technologies.

EEE - Electrical and Electronics Engineering

Involves electrical systems, energy management, and power generation technologies.

Mechanical Engineering

Explores mechanics, thermodynamics, and material science for machine and device design.
